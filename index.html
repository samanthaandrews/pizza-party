Our Story

We started Pizza Party when all three of us, Jessica, Lizzy, and Amy, realized there weren't many ways to get creative and have fun without spending a lot of money going to the movies or going to a bar. Reminiscing on our childhood, we all agreed that time spent cooking with family and friends made for the best memories. Our love for fresh, delicious ingredients led us to create Pizza Party, where we help you have more fun in the kitchen.
